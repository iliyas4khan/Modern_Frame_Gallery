About The Modern Frame

Celebrating contemporary art since 1995

Our History

Founded in 1995 by art enthusiast Margaret Thompson, The Modern Frame began as a small gallery space dedicated to showcasing emerging contemporary artists. Over nearly three decades, we have grown into one of the city's premier destinations for modern art, while maintaining our commitment to discovering and nurturing new talent.

Our gallery has been home to over 200 exhibitions, featuring works from both local and international artists. We pride ourselves on creating a space where art lovers can discover new perspectives and where artists can share their vision with the world.

Located in the heart of the arts district, our 3,000 square foot space features rotating exhibitions, a permanent collection, and educational programs designed to make contemporary art accessible to everyone.

Notable Artists

Maria Santos

Abstract painter known for her vibrant color palettes and dynamic compositions. Featured in our permanent collection since 2010.

David Chen

Contemporary sculptor working with mixed media. His installations have been exhibited internationally.

Elena Rodriguez

Digital artist and photographer exploring themes of identity and technology in the modern world.

James Mitchell

Conceptual artist whose thought-provoking installations challenge viewers to reconsider their relationship with space.
