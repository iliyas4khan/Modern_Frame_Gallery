Directions

We are located in the heart of the Arts District, easily accessible by public transportation and with street parking available. The gallery is wheelchair accessible with elevator access to our second-floor location.

By Metro: Take the Blue Line to Arts District Station (2 blocks away)

By Bus: Routes 15, 23, and 45 stop directly in front of our building

Parking: Metered street parking and paid lots available nearby
